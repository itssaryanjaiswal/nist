About Us

Welcome to the Nistha Institute of Science & Technology, located in Devpar Bhawarnath, Azamgarh, Uttar Pradesh. Our institute is proud to be affiliated with Makhanlal Chaturvedi University, Bhopal, MP. We are dedicated to providing quality education in the fields of science and technology, preparing our students to meet the challenges of the modern world.

At Nistha Institute, we believe in fostering an environment where innovation, creativity, and critical thinking are encouraged. Our curriculum is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in today's rapidly evolving technological landscape. With state-of-the-art facilities and a commitment to excellence, we strive to nurture the next generation of scientists, engineers, and technologists.

Join us at Nistha Institute of Science & Technology and embark on a journey of discovery and learning. Together, let's shape the future of technology and contribute to the development of society.
